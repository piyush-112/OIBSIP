The life journey of Mahatma Gandhi :

  • 1869 - Born in porbandar,gujrat,india
  • 1879 - Enter local school for studies
  • 1883 - Married to Kasturba Makhanji
  • 1893 - Civil right movement in South Africa
  • 1909 - Leo tolstoy letter to ghandi
  • 1918 - Champaran Movement
  • 1930 - Dandi Salt march
  • 1942 - Quit india movement
  • 1948 - Died in New delhi,India
